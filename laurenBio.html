Lauren Pass


    Lauren Pass is a summa cum laude graduate of Howard University, where she obtained a Bachelor of Science in Biology. Lauren was the only African American in her class to obtain a Doctorate of Dental Surgery from Virginia Commonwealth University in Richmond, VA. During dental school, she served on the admissions committee and as a teaching assistant. Currently, she is completing her orthodontic residency at Columbia University where she is Chief Resident. Although Lauren is originally from Indianapolis, IN, she is a city girl at heart and currently resides in Manhattan. In her spare time, she enjoys the arts, live music, and traveling. She is a proud member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority.
